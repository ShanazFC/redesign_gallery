La Fromagerie Redesign

La Fromagerie is a bistro, wine bar, and retail wine store located in Old Town Alexandria. Their website was made using a website builder that made it difficult to display the ever-changing, dynamic content of a restaurant. I created a single-page layout that features a description, menus, upcoming events, regular events, and a gallery. Click the tabs below to see before and after images of each page.
